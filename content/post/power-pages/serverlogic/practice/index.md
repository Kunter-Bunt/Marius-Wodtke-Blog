This time we put Server Logic into practice to solve a practical problem: My custom Training Event entity offers both public and private events and allowing a Portal User to read all of them could leak information on the private events. Server Logic and Custom APIs will help us to retrieve the right amount of Training Events without assigning excessive privileges to our users.
